Add to your calendar - Google

  1. On your computer, open Google Calendar.
  2. On the left, next to "Other calendars," click Add Add other calendars and then From URL.
  3. Enter https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0?cid=a3ZndHJncm91cEBnbWFpbC5jb20
  4. Click Add calendar. The calendar appears on the left, under "Other calendars."

Add to your calendar - Apple IOS

  1. Launch the "Settings" app on your iPhone or iPad. The Settings screen appears.
  2. Choose "Passwords and Accounts" in the menu. The Passwords and Accounts screen is displayed.
  3. Tap "Add Account" in the Accounts section. The Add Account screen appears
  4. Choose "Other" in the list of options. The Other screen is displayed.
  5. Tap "Add Subscribed Calendar" in the list of options. The Subscription screen appears.
  6. Paste https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/kvgtrgroup%40gmail.com/public/basic.ics into the Server field, and then tap "Next." The information screen expands with information associated with the calendar. Do not change anything on the next scree but tap "Save." You'll be returned to the Passwords and Accounts screen.
  7. You can see your newly added shared Google calendar in your iPhone or iPad calendar by opening the Calendar app, and then tapping "Calendars" at the bottom of the screen. The Calendars screen is displayed. Scroll down to the Subscribed section, and you'll see your newly added calendar in this list. Ensure the circle to the left of that calendar has a check mark in it to see events from that calendar in your iPhone or iPad calendar.
